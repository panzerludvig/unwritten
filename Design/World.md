## Tone & Genre

Starting baseline: a **classic D&D fantasy world** — swords, magic, taverns, dungeons, and adventuring parties. Deliberately loose so play can begin immediately; specifics are dreamt up during play and recorded here as they solidify. See [[Design/Dungeon Master]] (The Setting) and [[Design/Overview]].
